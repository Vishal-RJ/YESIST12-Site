FAQs:

1. What is the purpose of sponsoring YESIST12?
Sponsoring the event provides a unique opportunity to showcase your brand to a targeted audience within the industry and community. By aligning your brand with our event, you gain visibility, credibility, and the chance to connect directly with your target audience.
2. What types of sponsorship opportunities are available?
We offer various packages such as Platinum Sponsor, Gold Sponsor, Silver Sponsor, and Bronze Sponsor, along with customizable options to fit different budgets and goals.
3. How can I become a sponsor?
Review the prospectus, identify your preferred package, and contact our team at sponsorship@ieeeyesist12.org to secure your sponsorship.
4. What is the expected ROI for sponsors?
Sponsors can expect increased brand visibility, direct access to a targeted audience, networking opportunities, and recognition as a supporter.
5. Are there opportunities for in-kind sponsorships?
Yes, we welcome in-kind sponsorships. Contact our team to explore options for integrating your products or services.
6. How will sponsors be recognized?
Sponsors receive visibility through digital promotion, marketing materials, social media, promotional stalls, workshops, and keynote speakers. Recognition level varies by package.
7. Can sponsors attend the event?
Yes, sponsors typically receive complimentary passes or discounted rates.
8. How can I get more information or discuss sponsorship details?
Contact our sponsorship team at sponsorship@ieeeyesist12.org for additional information and to tailor a sponsorship package that suits your goals.
